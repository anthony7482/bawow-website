Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Overview

Bawow ("we", "our", "the app") is a dog walking companion app. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

Data We Collect

We collect the following data when you use Bawow:

  • Account information: Email address and display name, used to create and manage your account.
  • Dog profile data: Dog name, breed, birthdate, and profile photo, provided by you to personalise the app experience.
  • Location data: GPS coordinates during active walk tracking only. We do not track your location in the background when you are not recording a walk.
  • Walk data: Walk routes, distance, duration, and timestamps, stored to provide your walk history and analytics.
  • Device information: App version and platform (iOS/Android) for crash reporting and compatibility.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide walk tracking, goal setting, and achievement features.
  • To enable multi-user dog co-ownership (household features).
  • To provide breed-specific walk recommendations.
  • To send walk reminders and achievement notifications (with your permission).
  • To detect and fix crashes via Sentry (anonymised crash reports).

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not serve advertisements.
  • We do not track your location when the app is closed or when you are not recording a walk.
  • We do not access your contacts, call history, or messages.

Third-Party Services

Bawow uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication. Data stored in the EU. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Google Maps: Map display during walk tracking. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • Sentry: Crash reporting (anonymised). Sentry Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers with row-level security policies. All network communication uses HTTPS encryption. Authentication tokens are stored in secure platform storage.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Export your data: Use the "Export My Data" feature in Settings to download all your data as a JSON file.
  • Delete your account: Use the "Delete Account" option in Settings. This permanently removes all your data from our servers.
  • Revoke permissions: You can revoke location, camera, and notification permissions at any time through your device settings.

Children's Privacy

Bawow is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at: privacy@bawow.app
